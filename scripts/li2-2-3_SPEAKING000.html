스피킹 하는 방법(1 day a week)
1. Choose the topic(from 'Random Topic Generator'  or  'language-exchange-topics')
2. Try to speak with one opinion and two or three grounds to support it.
3. Write down your opinion on notebook.
4. Read it aloud three times.
5. Speak again without script.(Do not try to remember, just tell)
